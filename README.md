# CRM-Lead-Opportunity-Dashboard
## 📊 CRM Sales Dashboard (Tableau Project)
**🚀 Overview**

This project showcases a Customer Relationship Management (CRM) Dashboard developed using Tableau to analyze leads, opportunities, and sales performance.

The dashboard is divided into two major sections:

**Lead Dashboard** – Focuses on lead generation and conversion tracking.

**Opportunity Dashboard** – Provides insights into revenue, deals, and sales performance.

This dashboard enables businesses to make data-driven decisions by identifying trends, patterns, and areas for improvement.

**🎯 Objectives**

Track lead generation from different sources.

Analyze conversion rates and sales funnel stages.

Monitor revenue trends and forecast performance.

Identify top-performing industries and opportunity types.

**📌 Features**

**🔹 Lead Dashboard**

Total Leads: 10K

Converted Accounts: 1,006

Converted Opportunities: 367

Conversion Rate: 8.95%

Expected Amount: ₹38.7K

**Visualizations Included:**

📊 Lead by Source (Bar Chart)

📊 Lead by Industry (Horizontal Bar Chart)

🍩 Lead Conversion (Donut Chart)

🔵 Lead by Stage (Bubble Chart)

🎛️ Filters: Lead Source, Status

**🔹 Opportunity Dashboard**

Total Opportunities: 1,579

Closed Deals: 160

Win Rate: 10.13%

Loss Rate: 89.87%

Expected Revenue: ₹59.14M

**Visualizations Included:**

📊 Expected Amount by Opportunity Type

📈 Revenue Trend (Line Chart)

📉 Forecast vs Expected Revenue (Area Chart)

📊 Top Industries by Opportunities

📊 Total vs Closed Opportunities

🎛️ Filters: Year, Account Type

**🛠️ Tools & Technologies**

Tableau – Dashboard development

Microsoft Excel – Data cleaning & preparation

Data Visualization & Storytelling

Dashboard Design & Interactivity

**📈 Key Insights**

Inside Sales and Website contribute the highest number of leads.

Safety & Security and Life Sciences industries generate maximum leads.

Conversion rate is relatively low → indicates improvement needed in lead nurturing.

High loss rate (89.87%) suggests optimization required in sales strategy.

Revenue trend shows fluctuations with peak performance in certain years.
